Search for a Parcel

To search for a parcel, enter the parcel ID in the search bar in the top right corner of the page. On small screens or mobile devices, you may have to expand the menu by touching the expand button.

Formatting

The format for a parcel ID is "###-##-###". You must enter the dashes when you search. Do not include any spaces or extra characters. Quotation must not be used.

Getting JSON information

A JSON version of the information posted on each parcel's page can be obtained by changing out the "/page/" portion of the URL to "/json/".
